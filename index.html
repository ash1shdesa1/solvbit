How We Work

From first call to live system

01

Discovery

1–2 week scoping sprint. We map your business problem, data assets, and constraints. You get a technical brief and a fixed-price proposal — no vague retainers.

02

Architect

We design the system before we write the first line of code. Architecture review, data flow diagrams, and a prototype that proves the core hypothesis.

03

Build

Two-week sprint cycles with weekly demos. Full test coverage, CI/CD from day one. You always have a working version you can inspect and redirect.

04

Launch & Scale

Deployment, monitoring, runbooks, and a handoff that your team can actually maintain. Optional retainer for ongoing improvement and scaling.
